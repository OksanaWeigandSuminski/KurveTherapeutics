Disease-Modifying Treatments for Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s and Other CNS Disorders

Kurve Therapeutics has proprietary, clinically-proven products offering a significant advance in the treatment of neurodegenerative diseases, enhanced by precision delivery of drugs to the brain. Clinical trials and several patient cases have demonstrated modification / reversal of neurodegenerative disease (e.g., Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s) as well as biomarker changes supporting Kurve products as disease-modifying treatments.
